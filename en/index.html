EU taxes

Ukraine has an agreement with the EU, according to which our EU wholesalers do not need to pay customs duties (customs tax). We give our wholesalers the EUR1 certificate, which confirms that the products are made in Ukraine. On the basis of this certificate, no customs duty is paid in the EU countries.
